## 1. Purpose

The purpose of this Security Policy is to protect the integrity, confidentiality, and availability of the Chaos community, its members, and our website. It defines how we safeguard information, prevent abuse, and ensure a safe environment for all members.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:

* Chaos website administrators and moderators
* Members participating in Chaos WhatsApp groups
* Any data voluntarily provided to Chaos through the website or group submissions

---

## 3. Information Security

We handle personal information (name, phone number, email, messages) with care:

* Data is stored securely by authorized administrators only
* Data access is limited to administrators for community management
* Data is never shared with third parties for marketing or other purposes

---

## 4. Website Security

We implement the following measures:

* Use of secure login methods for administrators
* Regular updates to website software to prevent vulnerabilities
* Monitoring for unauthorized access or suspicious activity
* Secure hosting environments

---

## 5. WhatsApp Group Security

To maintain safety in our WhatsApp groups:

* Only verified members are allowed to join
* Moderators monitor group activity for spam, harassment, or abuse
* Members are prohibited from sharing illegal or harmful content
* Violations may result in immediate removal from the group

---

## 6. Member Responsibilities

Members are expected to:

* Keep their personal login and account information private
* Avoid sharing sensitive information in group chats
* Report any suspicious activity to administrators immediately
* Follow all community rules

---

## 7. Incident Response

If a security incident occurs (e.g., unauthorized access, data exposure):

1. Administrators will investigate promptly
2. Affected members will be notified if their personal information is at risk
3. Appropriate measures will be taken to prevent recurrence

---

## 8. Data Retention and Deletion

* Personal data is retained only as long as necessary to manage the community
* Members can request deletion of their information at any time
* Upon removal from the community, data is securely deleted by administrators

---

## 9. Policy Updates

Chaos reserves the right to update this Security Policy at any time. Updates will be reflected by the “Last Updated” date.
